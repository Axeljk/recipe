Cheese Cupcakes

Ingredients

  • 24oz cream cheese (Philadelphia preferred)
  • 3 eggs
  • 2/3 cups of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 12 vanilla wafers (Nilla preferred)
  • 12 cupcake liners
  • 12oz fruit pie filling (or other topping)

Directions

Allow cream cheese to soften at room temperature.

Line cupcake pan. Place a vanilla wafer in each cup.

With electric mixer, beat cream cheese.

Add eggs, sugar, and vanilla. Continue to beat until thoroughly mixed.

Divide mixture among prepared cups.

Bake in preheated oven at 350Â°F for 17 minutes.

Top with fruit pie filling, or other topping of choice.

Refridgerate for 8 to 24 hours. Longer the better.

Yields 12, but can be stretched to 18 for smaller cakes.
